# SHAPE (Innovation Lab)

## Synopsis

The SHAPE offering focuses on rapid ideation, prototyping, and validation of innovative digital concepts. It accelerates experimentation through immersive technologies, design thinking, and structured innovation frameworks to help enterprises envision, test, and de-risk new digital initiatives.

## Indicative Use Cases by Industry

### Insurance

**Problem:** Agents struggle to explain complex products leading to lower conversions.
**Solution:** AR/VR immersive product walkthroughs to demonstrate coverage scenarios.

### Financial Services

**Problem:** New product ideas take too long to validate.
**Solution:** MVP prototyping and customer journey experiments to test demand.

### Retail

**Problem:** Low in-store engagement.
**Solution:** XR-based virtual try-ons and interactive product discovery.

### Automotive

**Problem:** Customers unable to visualize custom configurations.
**Solution:** 3D/VR vehicle configurators for dealerships.

### Manufacturing

**Problem:** Training for complex machinery is slow and risky.
**Solution:** AR-assisted training simulations and Digital Twin PoCs.

### Healthcare

**Problem:** Need for safe, simulated training environments.
**Solution:** VR-based clinical simulations and patient journey prototyping.
