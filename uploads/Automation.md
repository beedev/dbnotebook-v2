# MODERNIZE (Digital Operations)

## Synopsis

The MODERNIZE offering focuses on operational scalability and efficiency through automation, integration, and modern digital platforms. It modernizes enterprise backbones, reduces manual effort, and enables secure, API-driven, composable digital ecosystems.

## Details

* **Automation & Integration**

  * RPA, IPA, and BPM-based automation.
  * Low-code/no-code development to accelerate delivery.
  * API design, integration services, and platform interoperability.

* **Digital Platforms**

  * Commerce platforms for B2B/B2C.
  * Web Content Management Systems (WCMS).
  * SEO, GEO, and digital performance optimization.
  * Enterprise portal modernization.

## Indicative Use Cases by Industry

### Insurance

**Problem:** Legacy core systems slow business responsiveness.
**Solution:** API-based integration layers, automated underwriting/claims workflows, and portal modernization.

### Financial Services

**Problem:** Manual back-office operations cause delays and compliance risk.
**Solution:** RPA for reconciliation, workflow orchestration, and low-code apps for operations.

### Retail

**Problem:** Fragmented commerce systems impact conversion.
**Solution:** Unified commerce platforms, inventory sync, and SEO/experience optimization.

### Automotive

**Problem:** Dealer systems are siloed and manual.
**Solution:** Integrated dealer portals and automated service workflows.

### Manufacturing

**Problem:** Legacy ERP/SCM processes limit agility.
**Solution:** Automation of supply chain workflows and API-led integration across systems.

### Healthcare

**Problem:** Appointment, billing, and clinical ops are manual.
**Solution:** Workflow automation, integrated patient portals, and modern WCMS for health content.
