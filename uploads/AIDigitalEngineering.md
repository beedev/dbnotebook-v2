# HARNESS (Power of AI)

## Synopsis

The HARNESS offering enables enterprises to operationalize AI across products, processes, and customer interactions. It focuses on AI-enabled engineering, GenAI adoption, and intelligent automation to unlock productivity, personalization, and decision intelligence.

## Details

* **AI-Enabled Digital Engineering**

  * Custom AI-powered application development.
  * Conversational AI and Chatbots.
  * GenAI integration across enterprise systems.
  * AI-assisted development for faster SDLC cycles.

* **Intelligent Automation**

  * Process automation using AI/ML.
  * AI-driven marketing automation.
  * Predictive analytics for forecasting, risk scoring, and insights.

## Indicative Use Cases by Industry

### Insurance

**Problem:** Claims triage is manual and slow.
**Solution:** AI models for automated claims classification, fraud detection, and conversational FNOL.

### Financial Services

**Problem:** High operational load in compliance and reporting.
**Solution:** AI-driven document intelligence and automated KYC/AML workflows.

### Retail

**Problem:** Personalization is inconsistent across channels.
**Solution:** AI-powered recommendation systems and customer intent prediction.

### Automotive

**Problem:** After-sales support is resource-intensive.
**Solution:** Conversational AI for service diagnostics and proactive maintenance predictions.

### Manufacturing

**Problem:** Downtime causes major productivity loss.
**Solution:** Predictive maintenance using ML and anomaly detection on sensor data.

### Healthcare

**Problem:** Clinician time spent on documentation is high.
**Solution:** GenAI-based clinical documentation assistants and medical knowledge retrieval.
