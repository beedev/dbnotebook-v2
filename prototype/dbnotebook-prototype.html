What are the key pricing strategies we should use for enterprise deals in Q4?
DBNotebook 2:34 PM

Based on your Q4 Sales Strategy documents, here are the key pricing strategies for enterprise deals:

  1. Value-Based Pricing: Focus on demonstrating ROI rather than competing on price. Enterprise clients care more about outcomes than upfront costs.
  2. Multi-Year Discounting: Offer 15-20% discounts for 3-year commitments to improve LTV and reduce churn.
  3. Tiered Volume Pricing: Implement usage-based tiers that scale with the customer's growth, ensuring they see value at every stage.
  4. Bundle Services: Include implementation, training, and premium support in enterprise packages to differentiate from competitors.

The pricing guidelines also recommend avoiding discounts greater than 25% as it signals low product confidence.
